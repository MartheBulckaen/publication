TO SEE WITH YOUR EYES

I must see new things and investigate them. I want to taste dark water and see crackling trees and wild winds.

—Egon Shiele—

To be able to translate my vision of my work, I have used this quote by Egon Schiele. Especially the first sentence describes how I find my inspiration. It is interesting to discover new things and to use these in your work, as it were like you are 'stealing with your eyes'. I love things that don't scream for attention but that show only that what is necessary in order to understand them. In my training as a graphic designer, we work mainly on a base of assignments. Nevertheless, it is a matter to put things to your own hand and to give them a piece of yourself, but just what you know is not enough, therefore, you have to keep searching and never stop investigating.

Marthe B.
